## Platform Strategy

### Why Meta First
Neon Aura's services are highly visual — perfect for Instagram and Facebook feed/Stories. The target audience (small business owners, event planners, solopreneurs) is active on Meta and responds to strong visual creative. Meta's interest and behavior targeting lets us reach people at the exact moment they're thinking about their business brand.

### Why Google Search Second
People searching "logo design for small business" or "business card designer near me" have high purchase intent. These clicks convert well. Run this after Meta establishes some brand recognition.

## 30-Day Optimization Checklist

**Week 1:**
- Launch all campaigns
- Monitor delivery and CPM (should be $5–15 for Meta awareness)
- Check that conversion tracking is firing

**Week 2:**
- Pause any ads with CTR below 1% on Meta
- Check Google Quality Scores (target 7+)
- Add negative keywords if irrelevant traffic showing

**Week 3:**
- Scale budget 20% on best-performing Meta ad set
- Add retargeting audience if site traffic is building (500+ visitors)
- Test a new creative angle

**Week 4:**
- Full review: CPA vs. target
- Pause bottom 20% performers
- Plan next month's creative refresh
