## Target Audiences

### Primary — Small Business Owners
- Entrepreneurs launching or rebranding a business
- Age 25–45, service-based or product-based businesses
- Pain: Their current branding looks unprofessional or DIY
- Desire: A brand that looks legitimate and attracts clients
- Platforms: Instagram, Facebook

### Secondary — Event Planners & Hosts
- Wedding planners, event organizers, individuals planning milestone events
- Need: Beautiful invitations that set the right tone
- Pain: Generic templates from Canva look like everyone else's
- Platforms: Instagram, Pinterest

### Tertiary — Freelancers & Solopreneurs
- Consultants, coaches, photographers, real estate agents
- Need: Business cards and social media posts that build credibility
- Pain: No time or design skills to create visual content
- Platforms: LinkedIn, Instagram

## Campaign Phases

### Phase 1 — Awareness (Weeks 1–2)
**Goal:** Get in front of cold audiences
- Launch Meta awareness ads (hero + pain angles)
- Post 5x/week on Instagram with before/after content
- Launch TikTok with behind-the-scenes process video

### Phase 2 — Consideration (Weeks 3–4)
**Goal:** Warm audience, collect leads
- Retarget page visitors with social proof ads
- Launch lead magnet ("Free Brand Checklist")
- Begin email nurture sequence
- LinkedIn posts targeting solopreneurs

### Phase 3 — Conversion (Weeks 5–8)
**Goal:** Book design projects
- Retarget email list with direct offer ads
- Launch limited-time offer (e.g. "10% off logo package")
- Send conversion email (#5 in sequence)
- Run Google Search ads for "logo design service"

## Messaging Framework

### Core Value Proposition
> Professional brand design that makes small businesses look like they've been around for years — delivered fast, without the agency price tag.

### Pain Points Addressed
1. "My brand looks DIY and I'm losing clients because of it"
2. "I don't have time to design my own social posts"
3. "My invitations looked cheap and embarrassed me"
4. "I hand out business cards and people don't call back"

### Proof Points (to build as you gather them)
- Number of brands designed
- Client testimonials
- Before/after portfolio examples
- Turnaround time ("logos delivered in 3-5 days")
