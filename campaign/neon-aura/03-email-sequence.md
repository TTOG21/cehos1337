## Email 3 — Your Brand Is Losing You Money

**Send:** Day 4
**Subject:** 3 branding mistakes costing you clients right now
**Preview:** You're probably making at least one of these — here's how to spot them

---

Hey [First Name],

After working with hundreds of small businesses, we've seen the same three branding mistakes over and over. They're subtle. They're common. And they're quietly costing people clients.

**Mistake #1: The DIY logo**
Canva is great for a lot of things. A logo isn't one of them. When a prospect sees a generic template logo, it signals one thing: this business isn't established. Whether that's true or not doesn't matter — *perception is reality*.

**Mistake #2: No visual consistency**
Your Instagram is blue. Your business card is green. Your website is a different font altogether. When nothing matches, clients don't remember you — and they don't refer you.

**Mistake #3: Business cards that look like everyone else's**
A standard white card with black text gets glanced at and forgotten. A card with a great design gets *kept* — and sometimes photographed and shared.

Here's the good news: all three of these are fixable. Fast.

A professional logo, a consistent color palette, and a well-designed card can completely change how potential clients perceive your business — before you even speak.
