# Neon Aura Studio — Static Ad Image Prompts

## Brand Identity Reference
- **Background:** Deep navy #080D1A
- **Primary accent:** Cyan/teal #00E5FF
- **Secondary accent:** Purple/violet #7B2FE0
- **Logo:** Circle outline with cyan→purple gradient + 4-pointed star inside
- **Typography:** Bold white sans-serif, "STUDIO" in cyan
- **Tagline:** "We make brands glow"
- **Services:** Logos · Social Media Posts · Invitations · Business Cards
- **Aesthetic:** Dark, neon glow, premium, modern creative studio

---
